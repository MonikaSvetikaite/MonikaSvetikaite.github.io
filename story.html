On a calm Friday afternoon, you walk through the forest enjoying the calm surroundings. The sun is slowly setting, the mellow sunlight peeking through the leaves that are lightly moving with the wind. As you head home there is a change in the atmosphere an unsettling feeling like there is someone watching, the feeling only intensifies. Your heart starts racing, you pick up the pace and look back, the forest is starting to disappear into the darkness as the sun is now below the tree line and the wind is picking up.

You hear a twig snap, without looking back you start running but only after a few minutes you stop for a break. Leaning against a tree to calm the breathing, you look around and realize that you are lost, panic starts to kick in and then you spot a figure coming towards you. They seem to have something in their hand, you do not examine the person and turn to run, making their job easy by running into a tree and knocking yourself out.
